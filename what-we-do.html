Donation through Warmth

Sri Lanka is a small country, but it produces high demandable technologist each ear. And its famous for lot of unique innovative inventions.
But the lack of resource makes ideas as always ideas.

Our Plan

Our plan is to make a secure connection between the Donors around the world and Donee in Sri Lanka.
Through this
donors can satisfy that the donation is done for a trustable cause. And donee will the capability to ask the community to help them.

How this works

There are lot of institutions and individuals are looking for resource to succeed there dream project ideas. But they don't have any source to get the needful resources as a donation or with the low cost price. Through this portal, those people can send us a requirement application along with the reliable personal and project information. We will verify all the documents manually, by visiting physically to the place where the institution placed or place where research project take place. And then we will publish the requirement to our portal. With all the contact details.

How Donor Benefit

Donors can easily find the products that needs donations. And also they can trust the request of donee. Since all the requirements are analyzed properly. Also when ever a donor request about donee requirement documentation and review report. We can send those to the donor.

How Donee Benefit

Its very hard in Sri Lanka to find a donor for a certain requirements. But also there are several people who works out of Sri Lanka likes to donate something. But they don't have any source to view actually who needs donation. Now they randomly select some places and donate there. Which makes in equality in resource sharing. Through this portal we are gathering both people into one place, and facilitate them to communicate easily.
