WIFI Web Camera

Our project is related to image processing & robotics. Which a robotic vehicle with wifi camera capture images on his route and send those images to server. Server will process the image and send a signal to the robotic vehicle to automatically behave on its route. For this we need a WIFI web cam. We have gone through lot of retailers in Sri Lanka, but wi fi cams are very expensive here. So we are searching for donor to do our research further.

School Laboratory

We are from St Jhon Boscos College, Hatton. A small school in central part of Sri Lanka. We are looking to build a new IT room section, which facilitate small children to participate in computer studies. Currently we have 15 machines in our school but those machines are not enough to teach a class of students at one time. So we are looking for donors who can provide used or brand new computer items for our school.
