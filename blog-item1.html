School Laboratory

We are from St Jhon Boscos College, Hatton. A small school in central part of Sri Lanka. We are looking to build a new IT room section, which facilitate small children to participate in computer studies. Currently we have 15 machines in our school but those machines are not enough to teach a class of students at one time. So we are looking for donors who can provide used or brand new computer items for our school.
