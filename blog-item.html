WIFI Web Camera

Our project is related to image processing & robotics. Which a robotic vehicle with wifi camera capture images on his route and send those images to server. Server will process the image and send a signal to the robotic vehicle to automatically behave on its route. For this we need a WIFI web cam. We have gone through lot of retailers in Sri Lanka, but wifi cams are very expensive here. So we are searching for donor to do our research further.
